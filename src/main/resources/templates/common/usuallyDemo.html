查询操作栏

1.查询操作栏的布局结构(.bar)

(1) 操作栏用类名.bar
(2) 居中的表单操作都放在类名为"form-inline和action-bar"的form标签里
(3) 每个表单都需要单独放在.form-group的div里。

<div class="bar"> <form class="form-inline action-bar"> <div class="form-group"> <label>地市</label> <select class="form-control"> <option>云南</option> <option>昆明</option> </select> </div> <div class="form-group"> <label>日期</label> <div class="date" readonly="readonly"> <img class="date-img" src="../static/images/img/date.png"> <input class="layui-input" autocomplete="off"> </div> </div> <div class="form-group"> <input class="layui-input" /> </div> <button type="button" class="btn btn-borderless search"><img src="../static/images/img/search.png"></button> </form> </div>

<div class="bar"> <div class="back"> <a href="#"> <img src="../static/images/img/back.png"> 返回 </a> </div> <form class="form-inline action-bar"> <div class="form-group"> ... </div> <div class="form-group"> ... </div> <button type="button" class="btn btn-borderless search"><img src="../static/images/img/search.png"></button> </form> </div>
